About Me

Hi! My name is Jacob.

What are your favorite things to do?

I love to play soccer, ski, and hang out with my friends. I also like to watch sports on TV.

What is your favorite place you’ve traveled and why?

Aspen, because I love skiing and it’s a great place. Every year my family takes a trip during Spring Break so I often get to have my birthday out there. I like to hike to the top of the Aspen Highlands bowl, which is 12,392 feet high. The skiing down is awesome with lots of fresh powder.

Where have you been overseas that you’ve liked?

Paris. It’s a really cool place and the food is amazing and there is so much to do. We walked to the top of the Eiffel Tower, which has a great view of the city. We visited during the World Cup and I stayed up until 2 am watching the US vs Portugal game. I also liked visiting Holland because I enjoy biking and we got to explore by bike. The people there are so friendly.

What is something you’ve done in your life that you are very proud of?

Volunteering in Kenya and teaching the students there some of the things I know. We encourage them to be better students, even though they so little. I am very proud that we feed them lunch every school day – otherwise they wouldn’t eat all day.

I am also proud of the wilderness trips I take with Camp Chewonki. Last summer I kayaked 25 miles over the course of 5 days and slept on Maine coastal islands. I’m proud of it because we worked well together as a group even when the weather was terrible.

What is one hope you have for your future?

I’d like to get into a good high school. We haven’t started looking yet, but I’d like to be at place that has good academics and a good soccer team, and a place where I can make a lot of friends and have a great time.

What is the funniest joke you’ve heard lately?

Not one I’m willing to post on a website that my grandparents are going to be looking at.
